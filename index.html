What is included with every house cleaning?

Here is what you can expect from our RM professionals!

    Bedroom, Living, and Common Areas

  • Vaccum the carpet
  • Wash bedsheets and make the bed
  • Clean the blinds and window seals
  • Dust all accessible surfaces

    Kitchen Cleaning

  • Clean cabinets inside or out
  • Wipe the stove
  • Wipe inside and out of the microwave
  • Clean oven inside and out

    Bathroom Cleaning

  • Dust all accessible surfaces
  • Santitize and wash toilet, tub, shower, and sink
  • Wipe down all mirrors
  • Clean cabinets inside and out

    Additional

  • Remove spider webs
  • Clean light switches
  • Clean air vents (when needed)
  • Scrub baseboards

One-Time Clean


Maybe your home is overdue for a spring cleaning, you have guests visiting from out of town, or you need help cleaning up after a party.
Whichever the reason may be, the One-Time Clean option gives you the felxibility to clean your home on a less frequent basis. Rest assured our professional team will leave your house feeling freshly clean!

Recurring Cleaning


Cleanliness plays an important role in the comfort of your home. With regular, recurring cleaning you and your house will always be ready for whatever life throws your way expected or unexpected.
Whether you schedule your visits every week, every two weeks, or monthly, a recurring cleaning option ensures you the peace of mind you deserve.

Moving Cleaning


We know that moving alone is such a stressful time, having to pack boxes and loading the truck, you already have enough on your plate. So why add more stress?
Performing a deep clean before moving out is beneficial to both parties. RM House Cleaning Services offers flexible move-in and move-out cleaning services to ease the transition into your new space. Whether the entire house or just certain items need to be cleaned, our professional team is there to help.

Special Event Cleaning


Holidays are right around the corner and you are throwing your anual Christmas party, or hosting the biggest birthday party for a loved one, whichever the occasion may be we are ready and willing to fulfill your specific cleaning requests so you can focus on other important preparations and tasks.

Same Day Cleaning


With so much going on in everyday lives, we understand there is barely enough time throughout the day.
The good news is we are equipped to handle last-minute cleanings. Thanks to our staff size, equipment, and team organization we are ready to provide immediate help to those who need it.
Our company is designed to offer flexible cleaning services, whether it’s regular house cleanings, once in a while touch-ups, or down-and-dirty last-minute scrubbings. No matter how many times we go, we’ll leave your home feeling fresh and clean.

Customize Cleaning


With Customize Cleaning, we give you the felxibility to choose what areas need to be cleaned during each visit.
Maybe one week you just need the bathrooms cleaned or the next week you need the baseboards and window seals touched up, well with this service you can rest easy knowing you have that option.
